## 2. Data Collection & Storage

ShieldPress VPS **does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data** to our servers. The Software operates entirely on your own VPS and all data remains local to your server.

Specifically:

- **No personal data** is sent to ShieldPress servers.
- **No website content, database data, or configuration files** are transmitted externally.
- **No user credentials or authentication information** leaves your server.
- **No telemetry or usage data** is collected without your explicit consent.

## 3. Logs & Tracking

ShieldPress VPS includes optional logging and tracking functionality. This feature:

- Is **disabled by default**.
- Only activates **if you explicitly enable it** during installation or through the settings.
- Operates **solely on your own VPS** — logs are stored locally on your server.
- Can be **disabled at any time** through the ShieldPress admin panel.

You have full control over what is logged and tracked on your server.

## 5. Security

Since all data remains on your VPS, you are responsible for:

- Maintaining the security of your server environment.
- Keeping ShieldPress VPS up to date with the latest version.
- Securing SSH access and server credentials.
- Performing regular backups of your data.
